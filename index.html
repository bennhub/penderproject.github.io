The Projects

What is the Pender Project?

The Pender project is a bucketlist of home projects I want to complete


The idea is to offer my services in technology (mainly web development & content creation) in exchange for help on my home projects.

The back story
After several years of saving, I was finally able to save for a downpayment and purchased a property on Pender Island in 2021. It was a risk, most of my life savings went in to buying the property. I embrace challenge, and came up with an idea to help with keep my costs low for property projects. The Idea:
What if I offer my skills in exchange for their skills.

An example would be, I can build you a website, if you can help be build a Fence..or help assemble a shed.. etc
